  1. Consultation -We set some time aside to go over the details of what you want, what you need and how we can get you there

  2. Proposal- will create a detailed project proposal for you, along with visual aids to give you an idea of what the finished product will look like. We can fine tune the details from there and decide on the plan of action for getting it done.

  3. Creation -Once I've started working on your site, you'll get regular updates as I make progress and test features. Nobody gets left in the dark in this process.
